About Me

Hi, I’m Alex Morgan — a photographer driven by curiosity and light. For me, photography is not about what it is, but what it could become. I believe in the life. Images are born when people feel seen, when places breathe, and when emotions are allowed to unfold naturally.

Over the past 8 years, I’ve photographed intimate weddings, countless portraits, and journeys across three continents. Whether it’s a quiet glance, a chaotic dance floor, or a misty horizon — my goal is always the same: to preserve the essence of the moment.

  • Based In: New York, available worldwide
  • Experience: 8+ years
  • Published In: LensCulture, Modern Photography Journal
  • Studies: Visual Arts, Parsons School of Design

Philosophy

Photography is my language. I speak through the moments I capture. I don't aim to create staged pictures — I aim to craft timeless fragments of truth.
